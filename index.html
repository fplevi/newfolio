About

I work on end-to-end projects — from discovery to production delivery — collaborating with stakeholders, engineers, and business teams. I value craft and excel at interface development, especially in Figma.

I have been working with product since 2022, when I joined CASSI, a health plan serving over 400,000 beneficiaries inside one of Brazil's largest banks, Banco do Brasil. I started as a product designer, helping form the first UX team and structure initial processes. Due to my initiative and autonomy in delivering results, I soon took on the leadership of the team.

I am from Brasília, with over 10 years of experience in the design industry. Before working with product, I operated across several areas of design — mainly as a freelancer — serving publishers, agencies, software houses, and other companies. During this time, I designed brands, laid out books, newspapers, and magazines, and produced visual and video content for social media.

I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Graphic Design and a postgraduate specialization in Animation, Interactive Technology, Video Graphics and Special Effects from IESB, as well as an MBA in UX Research, ResearchOps and Design Leadership.
